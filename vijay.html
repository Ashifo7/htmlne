Mahendra Singh Dhoni (/məˈheɪndrə ˈsɪŋ dhæˈnɪ/ ⓘ; born 7 July 1981) is an Indian professional cricketer, who plays as a wicket-keeper-batsman. He was the captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. He is widely regarded as the greatest captain in India.[2][3] He plays for and do captaincy of Chennai Super Kings franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
